eventbyte is an ease-of-life software application designed to easily create and distribute tickets for events. Initially designed for the Department of Computer Science at the University of Warwick, the application was used for departmental events, and the occasional society social. Since its release, eventbyte has grown into the forefront of modern digital ticketing, having been used in over 20 timezones.

The application is built on Python, Flask, and Flask-SQLAlchemy using SQLite3, as well as the use of JavaScript and JQuery to boost the user experience.
